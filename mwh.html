Mary Wescott Higgins

Responsibility Branding, Print, Web
Studio We Are Charette
Art Director Emily Charette

Mary describes herself as a moderate Democrat. She believes government should be smart, efficient, and accessible to everyone. Advocacy for people who can not speak for themselves is her passion. Her political views have been shaped by her experience in working with and for citizens who are often overlooked in the political process.

She recently ran as a Democrat for the House of Representatives, District 82 of Florida, where she attempted to raise a grassroots effort to combat big sugar corporations and their lobbying efforts in the state of Florida, among other things.

The Identity

The mark incorporates Mary's three initals, MWH, along with a curve that represents the movement of a wave. The wave, located in the negative space of the mark, symbolizes Mary's passion for saving the waterways of Florida and also represents the wave of a grassroots following growing and creating change.
